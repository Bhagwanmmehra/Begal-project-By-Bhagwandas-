The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound,similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare,known as beagling.Processing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inherited health problems.
